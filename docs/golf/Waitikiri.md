# Waitikiri

**Waitikiri Golf Club** · 111 Waitikiri Drive · Par 71. A.R. Blank's 1930s parkland design, famous for its **13 dogleg holes**. Hole names, descriptions, doglegs and hazards are layered in from the club's official hole-by-hole guide; Blue/White distances verified against the club card. Par-3 clubs are computed from distance & temperature; par-4/5 default to driver. (Men's tees shown; the club also runs a forward tee.)

**Phone** 03 383 1400 · **Web** www.waitikirigolf.co.nz
