# McLeans

**McLeans Island Golf Club** · 800 McLeans Island Road · Par 72. Tee clubs are for **calm conditions**, recalculated for each tee & temperature. In wind, adjust per the **[[Reference - Temperature and Wind|Reference - Temperature and Wind]]** page — nor'wester on hot days, cold southerly on cold days.

**Phone** 03 359 9768 · **Web** www.mcleansislandgolf.co.nz

## Front Nine (Out)

**Hole 1 — Heasleys** · Par 4 · S.I. 13

| Tee | Dist (m) | 6°C | 13°C | 20°C | 27°C |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Blue | 335 | 3w | 3w | 3w | 3w |
| White | 331 | 3w | 3w | 3w | 3w |
| Yellow | 303 | Dr | Dr | Dr | Dr |

> **3-wood off Blue/White, driver off the forward Yellow.** The right fairway bunker (~180 m) sits near your driver carry off the back tees, so lay to 3-wood and take the short wedge; off Yellow the carry is easy, so driver. Only fairway trouble, and on your safe side — your hook miss is left with no left bunker to punish it. The deep trap short-**left** of the green is the one to respect; left pin → aim slightly right and let the slope work.

## Back Nine (In)

**Hole 10 — Mountain View** · Par 4 · S.I. 14

| Tee | Dist (m) | 6°C | 13°C | 20°C | 27°C |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Blue | 317 | Dr | Dr | Dr | Dr |
| White | 315 | 3w | 3w | 3w | 3w |
| Yellow | 301 | Hyb | Hyb | Hyb | Hyb |

> **Driver off Blue, 3-wood off White, hybrid off Yellow** — the shorter the tee, the less you need, to leave a full wedge. No fairway bunker; your hook miss is the only watch (long-left is dead). A drive sliding **right** flirts with the silver birches but that's your safe side. Don't miss pin-high right — the gully there is a hard up-and-down.
