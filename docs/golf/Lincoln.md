# Lincoln

**Lincoln Golf Club** · Boundary Road, Lincoln (~25 min SW) · Par 68. A 9-hole course played twice for 18 (holes 1=10, 2=11, etc.); a few back-nine tees differ. Hole names, pars, stroke indexes and White distances (metres) are from the club's official scorecard. The club site shows photos rather than layout maps, so doglegs and hazards aren't shown (diagrams are schematic).

**Phone** 03 325 2453 · **Web** www.lincolngolf.co.nz
