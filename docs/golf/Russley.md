# Russley

**Russley Golf Club** · 428 Memorial Ave · Par 72. Parkland course with a back nine redesigned by Greg Turner. Pars, stroke indexes, Black/Blue/White/Yellow distances and per-hole descriptions, doglegs & hazards are from the club's official course guide. Holes are unnamed on the card. Par-3 clubs are computed from distance & temperature; par-4/5 default to driver.

**Phone** 03 358 4748 · **Web** www.russleygolfclub.co.nz
