# Everglades

**Everglades Country Golf Club** · 637 Marshlands Road (~20 min N) · Par 64. A short 9-hole parkland course played twice for 18 (est. 1967), bordered by the Kaputone Stream. Pars, stroke indexes and the men's tee are from a public scorecard database. **Note:** distances are entered as metres for consistency but directory listings quote the same figures as yards — treat the exact numbers as approximate. Schematic diagrams.

**Phone** 03 323 8641 · **Web** canterburygolf.co.nz/golf_club/everglades-golf-club
