# Christchurch GC

**Christchurch Golf Club** (Shirley Links) · 45 Horseshoe Lake Road · Par 72. NZ's second-oldest club (1873). Pars, stroke indexes, Black/Blue/White distances and per-hole descriptions, doglegs & hazards are layered in from the club's official course guide. The holes are unnamed on the card. Par-3 clubs are computed from distance & temperature; par-4/5 default to driver.

**Phone** 03 385 9506 · **Web** www.christchurchgolf.co.nz
