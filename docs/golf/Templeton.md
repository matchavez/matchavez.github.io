# Templeton

**Templeton Golf Club** · Pound Road · Par 73. Hole names, pars, stroke indexes and Blue/White/Yellow distances are from the club's official guide (the routing was renumbered from the old public-database version). The club site shows hole photos rather than layout maps, and its written hole descriptions are mismatched on some holes, so doglegs and hazards aren't shown (diagrams are schematic).

**Phone** 03 349 7493 · **Web** www.templetongc.co.nz
