# SAP Release Schedule Transition

---

> Purpose: Ensure that SAP CAB provides the right amount of support moving towards BAU.

## As is:

Daily "Emergency" CAB meetings at 1400, with every anticipation that this will be reduced.

## To be:

After 2-4 weeks, when the amount of changes wanes, daily CABs will be dropped in favour of weekly.

- Maintain 1400 booking daily for a natural ECAB time for the remainder of Hypercare.
- Final scheduled daily should fall on a Thursday, cancel Friday.
- Notify everyone that changes must be presented in writing by 1200 Monday each week.
- Weekly SAP CAB will occur at 1330 as the second-half hour of the regularly scheduled hour of CAB. Those that want to drop or add for CAB based on the type can do so, with Mat and Meredith staying at a minimum for both. Other changes get presented per normal at 1300 Wednesdays.
- Regular deployment will occur post meeting for those items that have passed and do not require any downtime.
- Security updates and any other items requiring downtime will be set for 1700 Wednesday by default, but will *always* have a discussion about scheduling, comms, and impact.

### Notable:

This approach has the endorsement of Andy and Accenture as a move to BAU. Andy concurs that weekly is generally preferred.

A fortnightly schedule might also be appropriate eventually, particularly if development is cycled in that timeframe for sprints, etc.
